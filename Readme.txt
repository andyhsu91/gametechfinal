Controls
---------------------------------------------------
	- WASD moves your robot
	- 'F' does shooting motion
	- Mouse movement changes the direction the camera is looking
	- 'C' turns off and on mouse to click buttons
	- 'M' is mute
	- 'P' is pause
	- 'Esc' quits the game
